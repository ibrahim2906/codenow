Privacy Policy

We are committed to transparency. This policy explains exactly how your data is handled technically.

Data Collection & Storage

We store the following data using Google Firebase:

  • Account Info: Email address (via Google Sign-In).
  • User Content: Tasks, notes, books, and courses you create.
  • File Links: URLs to files you upload (not the files themselves, see below).

AI Assistant & API Keys

To use the AI features, you must provide your own Google Gemini API Key.

Important Disclaimer:

If you use a Free Tier API key from Google, your conversation data may be used by Google to improve their products and train their models, according to Google's Terms of Service. We (How Much App) do not read or store your conversations on our servers, but they are processed by Google.

* Chat history is stored locally on your device (Browser Local Storage) for convenience.

File Uploads & Deletion

Storage Provider: We use Cloudinary as a third-party service to host your uploaded files (images, PDFs, audio).

Deletion Policy:

When you delete a file from the app (or empty the trash), the file is unlinked from your account interface immediately. However, due to technical caching, the raw file might remain accessible via its direct link on Cloudinary's servers for a period of time before being completely purged.

Please do not upload highly sensitive personal documents (e.g., IDs, financial records).

Security & Access

Transmission: All data is transmitted over a secure HTTPS connection (SSL).

Access Control: We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure that only you can access your own data and files.

Note: While we implement standard security measures, no web application is 100% immune to breaches. Use strong passwords for your Google Account.

Local Data & Public Devices

We store some data (like settings and chat history) in your browser's Local Storage to improve performance.

Warning: If you are using a public computer, please make sure to clear your browser cache/data after logging out to ensure your data is removed from the device.

Your Rights

  • You own your data. We do not sell it to advertisers.
  • You can export/download your files manually.

Contact

For privacy concerns or data deletion requests:

Contact Support via WhatsApp

Note: We will try to reply to you as soon as possible.
